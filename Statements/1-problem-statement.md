Problem Statement

Enterprises, government contractors, and regulated organizations often need to give vendors, subcontractors, auditors, and internal engineers limited access to sensitive cloud systems, logs, APIs, or operational tools.

The problem is that this access is usually handled badly:

long-lived IAM roles
over-permissioned accounts
broad VPN access
manual approvals in email or chat
weak audit trails
inconsistent logging
no clean evidence for compliance reviews
no time-bound access enforcement
poor visibility into who accessed what, when, why, and under whose approval

This creates real business risk:

unauthorized access
third-party exposure
failed audits
slow incident investigations
weak accountability
difficulty proving least privilege and zero-trust controls

The organization needs a system that gives the right person the right access to the right resource for the right amount of time, with full logging, approval, evidence, and policy enforcement.
