8. Compliance alignment targets

You are not certifying the platform.
You are designing it to be aligned to enterprise and government-style controls.

Use these as your control backbone:

NIST 800-53
FedRAMP Moderate-aligned design
NIST CSF
CISA Zero Trust Maturity Model

Focus your control mapping mostly on:

AC Access Control
AU Audit and Accountability
CM Configuration Management
IA Identification and Authentication
IR Incident Response
RA Risk Assessment
SC System and Communications Protection
SI System and Information Integrity
